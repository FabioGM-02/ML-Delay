***Factors affecting construction duration***
Authors: Kryukov K.
Department of Construction Management, Don State Technical University
Contact Information: kkrioukov@gmail.com

***Methodological information***
The purpose of the study was to determine the causes of construction delays in Russia.
Based on the review of existing literature, a list of the most commonly used factors affecting delays in construction projects was established. A group of experts was recruited to understand the key factors specific to Russia. As a result, the final composition of factors (with amount 32) affecting construction duration was obtained. The data in the database are the result of a questionnaire survey among participants in the investment and construction process. The survey questions used a 5-point Likert scale to measure responses.
